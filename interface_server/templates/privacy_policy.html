Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/11/2024

At PetMatters, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you visit and use our website.

Information We Collect

When you use PetMatters, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Includes information you provide, such as your name, email address, phone number, and details about your pet.
  • Pet Information: Details about your pet, including breed, age, gender, personality traits, special features, and any photos you upload.
  • Usage Data: Automatically collected information about your use of our website, like IP address, browser type, and browsing activity.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies to enhance your experience, track usage patterns, and improve services. You can adjust your browser settings for cookies.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Our Services: Create and manage your pet's profile, process forms, and enhance descriptions with AI tools.
  • To Improve Our Website and Services: Analyze usage data to improve website functionality and user experience.
  • To Communicate with You: Send updates, offers, and other communications related to your pet's profile and our services (opt-out available).
  • To Ensure Security and Legal Compliance: Prevent fraud, protect website security, and comply with legal obligations.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share your information in the following ways:

  • With Service Providers: Shared with third-party providers who help deliver our services (e.g., hosting, AI processing).
  • Legal Compliance: Disclosed if required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business Transfers: Transferred in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access and Correction: Request access to or correction of your personal information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal exceptions.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a commonly used format.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us to request deletion.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted with an updated effective date. Please review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
