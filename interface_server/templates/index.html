Introducing PetMatters: Providing Shelter Pets with the Attention They Need

We at PetMatters are committed to assisting animal shelters worldwide in finding the ideal match for their furry residents because we think every pet deserves a loving home. Our goal is clear: to assist more animals find their forever homes by giving animal shelters the resources they need to write appealing, factual pet descriptions in a variety of languages.


By offering a clever, AI-powered platform that eliminates the guesswork involved in creating engaging pet profiles, we hope to ease your workload. Our platform is made to support you at every stage, regardless of whether you're beginning from scratch or need to improve an already-written description.

How It Operates

Create a Draft Description

Write a preliminary description of the pet first. Just enter the essential information: breed, age, sex, personality features, and the kind of animal (dog, cat, etc.). You may also include any additional data that will assist in characterizing the pet.

Request to Make Your Description Better

You can ask our algorithm to make your description better once you've written it. Without sacrificing any important facts, our AI will refine your writing to make it more interesting and appealing. The outcome? A description that is more likely to attract prospective adopters' attention.

Make a New Description

Lacking the time to compose a description? No issue! Just enter the necessary details, such as the animal's breed, age, sex, and personality, and our AI will create a fresh, interesting pet description for you.

Translate to Dutch

Do you want to connect with more people? Any generated description can be readily translated from English to Dutch using our technology. For shelters hoping to reach Dutch-speaking adopters and improve their chances of finding the perfect home for their animals, this option is ideal.

Use and Share the Updated Description

You can use your description for flyers, your pet's internet page, and any other promotional materials once it is completed and translated, if needed. To assist your pet find a loving home more quickly, share it with prospective adopters, on your website, and on social media.
